Bourne Grammar School (BGS) is a co-educational grammar school with academy status on South Road (A15), in Bourne, Lincolnshire, England. The school was founded in 1330. It previously held Arts College Status, and was awarded Academy status in January 2012, although it retains its former name..

What's new

  • New school canteen

    The Chill (open from 08:15 until 16:00), The Gecko and The Plaza (both open at break and lunch times). The Hot Range — If you fancy something hot and comforting to warm you through, you will not be disappointed. For those who need a quick lunch or snack there is the hot grab and go selection; panini, hot wraps and baguettes, wedges, pizzas and pasta pots, all freshly made. For a more traditional lunch, there are the hot main meals; different every day (always including a vegetarian option) with influences from around the world. The Cold Range — If a lighter bite for lunch is preferred then the chiller cabinet has a selection of baguettes, bloomers, wraps, sandwiches, salads and pasta pots. To finish there are cold desserts and homemade cakes and cookies baked freshly each morning. Seating is provided in The Chill and The Gecko along with plenty of outdoor seating under one of three covered canopies.
